Campus Hours for Fall Break 2018

October 22nd, 2018

Classes will be dismissed on Thursday, Oct. 25, and Friday, Oct. 26, for fall break. UA offices will be open, but various areas on campus may have adjusted operating hours for those days.

Also check the UA Events Calendar for a list of activities occurring on campus during the break.

Bama Dining

For Bama Dining locations’ hours of operation, go to Bama Dining, select Hours, and click on Today’s Hours or Full Calendar.

Capstone Center for Student Success – Academic Support

Wednesday, Oct. 24, through Friday, Oct. 26, the Capstone Center for Student Success will be open 8 a.m. to 4:45 p.m. at the Russell Hall location, but there will be no tutoring available on those days. The Osband Hall location will not be open for any services on those days. Visit the third-floor Russell Hall location for study hall hours.

Crimson Ride

Crimson Ride will provide regular service from 7 a.m. to 9 p.m. on Thursday, Oct. 25, and Friday, Oct. 26, but with reduced coverage. All campus routes (Blue, Bronze, Crimson, Crimson Express, Gold 1, Gold 2, Green, Silver 1 and Silver 2) will run with a single bus; none of the rocket routes will run.

Apartment shuttles will have buses on standby; students should contact 348-7433 for a bus to get them and bring them to the Hub.

348-RIDE will provide regular service from 9 p.m.-7 a.m. on Thursday, Oct. 25, and Friday, Oct. 26, and all day Saturday and Sunday. Call 348-7433 with questions.

Counseling Center

Hours and services will continue as normal during the break.

Ferguson Student Center

Thursday, Oct. 25 through Friday, Oct. 26, 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. Saturday, Oct. 27, closed Sunday, Oct. 28, closed Regular schedule resumes Monday, Oct. 29

Library hours

Bruno, Gorgas and McLure libraries Wednesday, Oct. 24, 7:30 a.m. to 10 p.m. Thursday, Oct. 25, through Friday, Oct. 26, 7:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday, Oct. 27, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Regular schedule resumes Sunday, Oct. 28.

Rodgers Library Wednesday, Oct. 24, midnight to 10 p.m. Thursday, Oct. 25, through Friday, Oct. 26, 7:30 am. to 5 p.m. Saturday, Oct. 27, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Regular schedule resumes Sunday, Oct. 28.

Hoole Special Collections Thursday, Oct. 25, 9 a.m. to 4:45 p.m. Regular schedule resumes Friday, Oct. 26.

Sanford Media Center Wednesday, Oct. 24, 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. Thursday, Oct. 25, through Friday, Oct. 26, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday, Oct. 27, closed all day Regular schedule resumes Sunday, Oct. 28.

Museums

The Alabama Museum of Natural History, Gorgas House, Moundville Archaeological Park, Paul R. Jones Museum and Warner Transportation Museum will operate on regular schedules during fall break.

Parking

All safety rules and regulations apply as normal during fall break. Students should not park in faculty/staff or reserve surface lots.

Commuter: park in any commuter zone during fall break.

Residential: park in any non-gated residential zone during fall break.

Student Health Center and Pharmacy

The Student Health Center and Pharmacy will close at 5 p.m. Thursday, Oct. 25, and be on regular schedule Friday, Oct. 26. It will be closed Saturday, Oct. 27, through Sunday, Oct. 28.

Supply Store

The Ferguson Center location of the Supply Store will be closed Saturday, Oct. 27, and Sunday, Oct. 28. Otherwise, all locations will maintain regular hours.

University Recreation

The Competitive Sports Office will be closed Wednesday, Oct. 24, through Friday, Oct. 26. The Outdoor Recreation Rental Center and Bike Shop and SRC Climbing Wall will be closed Thursday, Oct. 25, through Sunday, Oct. 28.

A complete list of fall break hours for the Student Recreation Center and pool, Robert E. Witt Student Activity Center, and the Aquatic Center pool and weight room is available online.
