October 15th, 2018

UA Rural Medical Scholars Welcomes New Class

Ten students from rural Alabama who plan to become physicians practicing in rural communities in the state have been accepted into The University of Alabama’s Rural Medical Scholars Program for the 2018-19 academic year. The Rural Medical Scholars Program, which is part of UA’s College of Community Health Sciences, is a five-year medical education program that includes a year of study after students receive their undergraduate degree.

Honors College Recognized by Main Street America

Main Street Alabama has awarded The University of Alabama Honors College and Main Street Marion an Excellence in Public/Private Partnerships Award. This award recognizes outstanding local partnerships between Main Street and another organization.

Homecoming 2018

Katie Buhai of San Diego, a senior elementary education major, was named Homecoming Queen 2018. Students celebrated the week with a day service and painted the town red, not to mention pomping their yard displays, a parade, and the actual homecoming game, which the Tide won 39-10. Roll Tide!
